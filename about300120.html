Hello,

I am Julia, an Email developer & designer based in SE London, UK. I hand code Master Email temlates with the hybrid fluid method using less media queries. The modular and easy structure allows to create different versions of Email simply by putting them together as preferred. The templates render on most devices and in most popular ESPs. My focus is to give the templates some personal touch by experimenting with techniques such as illustration and keeping them as simple and accessible as possible.
